GPA-APP

Project Members: Brian Guo

Project Architecture: MEAN STACK

Front End: Angular, HTML/CSS, Node.JS and Express

Backend: MongoDB

Description: At UNC, there really isn't a built-in system for students to tracker and project their GPA (which they have to calculate by hand). The app is designed for students to better track their academic progress just by simply adding classes they have taken, the class weight, and their respective grades. Users are able to add, remove, and update classes they have taken before, which will dynamically change their GPA in the profile section.
